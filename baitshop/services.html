Fishing Line Spooling Service

At Howard's, we understand how important it is to have a successful and enjoyable fishing experience. Let our trained experts respool your reels to get you ready for your next big catch.

When you purchase a reel from us we provide the initial spooling service at no cost to you. Up to 200 yards of line from our bulk spools.

We charge no labor fees for other spooling services. You can bring your own line or pay us for the cost from our bulk spools.
